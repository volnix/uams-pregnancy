Over the Counter Pregnancy Medication Guide

It is always best to limit medication use during pregnancy if possible, especially during the first trimester. If medication is needed it is best to be conservative with dosing/frequency.

Nicotine
Women who are breastfeeding are encouraged not to smoke. The amount of nicotine from nicotine replacement products into breast milk is unknown. Use with caution.
